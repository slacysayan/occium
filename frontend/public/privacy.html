Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Overview

Occium ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Occium Content Pipeline.

2. Data Collection (Google & YouTube)

Occium uses Google OAuth to access your YouTube channel. We only request permissions necessary to:

  • View your channel metadata for identification.
  • Upload videos you explicitly select to your channel.
  • Manage scheduled publication times as requested by you.

We do not store your video content on our servers beyond the temporary duration required for the upload handoff. Once a video is successfully transferred to YouTube's servers, the local copy is deleted.

3. Data Collection (LinkedIn)

Occium accesses your LinkedIn profile name and picture to display within the app. We post content to your LinkedIn account only when you explicitly trigger the "Post Now" or "Schedule" action.

4. Data Storage

Most application data is stored locally in your browser's persistent storage (LocalStorage). Account tokens are used solely for the intended distribution purposes and are not shared with third parties.

5. Your Choices

You can disconnect your YouTube or LinkedIn accounts at any time through the "Accounts" page in the application, which will revoke our access immediately.
